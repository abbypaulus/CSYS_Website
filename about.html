How big is CSYS?

CSYS is the largest sports league in Coral Springs with approximately 2,500 participants each season. We are very proud to be recognized as the Official Recreational Soccer League in Coral Springs for over 50 years.

We run separate divisions for boys and girls, and have separate divisions based on age groupings. See our registration page for the current breakdown. Each team has between 1 and 2 volunteer coaches, usually parents, who receive various training courses provided by CSYS. We typically have 200 teams, over 450 coaches and hold over 1600 games.

When / How long is our season?

Click here to see our current season calendar. Online registration begins during the summer months. In-person registration begins at the end of August. Practices begin approximately 10 days before Thanksgiving. After a few weeks of practices, weekend games begin the first weekend of December. We break during the holiday period (schools closed) at the end of December and begin again after New Years Day.

For the 4 1/2 - 8 year olds, the season continues with games each weekend until the end of February. On the final day all of the players receive recognition.

For the older players, the regular season ends about mid-February. There are no practices for any team after this date. At this time, ALL of the teams go into the Soccer Bowl Championship, a tournament, which basically becomes a short second season. During this 3- week period, we play games every day of the week. Teams will have anywhere from 3 to 7 games during this period depending upon their ability to advance in the tournament. The season ends on the first Saturday in March with the Soccer Bowl Championship and Awards.

How to I sign up?

To sign-up for the CSYS program, you may register online at csys.org or visit one of our in-person registration sessions. We have three - The dates are:

  • Sat, August 26th
  • Sat, September 9th
  • Thu, September 14th

See the registration page for times and locations.

A $10.00 per player late fee will be assessed starting on December 31st

On-line registration is also available, with payment via credit card. See the registration page for details.

In-person registration is easy and fast if you come prepared:

  • All registrants must provide proof of Coral Springs residency at each registration. A utility bill is recommended. All NON-RESIDENTS MUST attend one of the in-person registrations. The City of Coral Springs charges a NON-RESIDENT FEE for each player. However, NON-RESIDENT players who attend Coral Springs schools do not have to pay the fee. Bring school record with player name. The NON-RESIDENT FEE is $55.00
  • FIRST TIME PLAYERS: If you are signing up for the first time, you will need a copy of a birth certificate, passport or other LEGAL document containing the child's date of birth. Returning players do not need this as the information is kept on file in our computer system.
  • A parent or legal guardian is required to sign the registration form for players under 18 years of age.
  • We prefer credit card, yet a check payable to "Coral Springs Youth Soccer" or "CSYS" in the proper amount is accepted at each registration. Cash is also acceptable. See fees below.
  • (PLEASE NOTE) The City of Coral Springs requires all participants in city sports leagues to pay a Sports Participation Fee at registration time. The current City of Coral Springs Sports Participation Fee for Soccer is $19.00 per child, which we have included in the registration fee. Please note that this may change. The Sports Participation Fee is a City of Coral Springs requirement and is not associated with Coral Springs Youth Soccer. We simply are collecting the required Sports Participation Fee for the City and passing directly to them.

What you will need to provide/purchase for your child to play?

  • Shin-guards. These are required for practices and games. Under no circumstances may players play or practice without shin-guards worn properly.
  • Soccer cleats or appropriate shoes. While we do permit players to play in regular sneakers, we do not recommend it as sneakers do not have any traction. Metal cleats or studs are also not appropriate for CSYS soccer.
  • We strongly recommend each player bring a ball of the appropriate size to each practice. It is also great to have around home to practice with. U6 and U8 use a number 3 size ball. U10 and U12 use a number 4. Older players use the regulation size number 5 ball.
