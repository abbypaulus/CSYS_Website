Coral Springs Youth Soccer truly appreciates the volunteer hours put in by our 400+ coaches each year. Without your dedication we could not have such a successful program. Each year we are in need of many new coaches, in addition to the returning 'regulars'. Its not hard - we provide free training courses. Just keep reading on.

Concussion Awareness

CSYS joins the sports community in expressing concern about the effects of head injuries on atheletes, so we are providing the following information to our coaches. We ask that all coaches complete the CDC training: CDC Training Course A short online course on recognizing and handling possible concussions. BHCS Presentation A shorter presentation from Broward Health Coral Springs on the Head Strong Concussion Care Program.
